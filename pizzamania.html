About Pizza

Welcome to Pizzamania, where we're crazy about pizza! Our family-owned restaurant has been serving up delicious, homemade pizzas since 1998. It all started with a passion for pizza and a desire to bring something truly special to our community. At Pizzamania, we believe that great pizza starts with the best ingredients. That's why we use only the freshest, locally sourced produce and high-quality meats and cheeses. Our pizzas are made from scratch every day, and we never cut corners when it comes to quality. Our menu features a wide variety of pizzas, from classic margherita to our signature Meat Lover's pie. We also offer delicious salads, appetizers, and desserts to round out your meal. Behind every great pizza is a great team, and our team is the best in the business. Our chefs and cooks have years of experience crafting the perfect pizza, and our friendly staff is always ready to welcome you with a smile. Come visit us at our cozy location in the heart of downtown, or order online for delivery or pickup. We're open seven days a week, and we can't wait to share our love of pizza with you. Connect with us on social media to stay up to date on our latest specials and events. Thanks for choosing Pizzamania!

Pizza Types

Cheezy Pizza

A pizza topped with a blend of four different cheeses such as mozzarella, provolone, Parmesan, and Romano, for a rich, cheesy flavor.

Family Pizza

A pizza with all the toppings - pepperoni, sausage, onions, green peppers, mushrooms, and black olives - along with a generous amount of mozzarella cheese.

Pepperoni Piza

A traditional pizza topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices.

Veggie Pizza

A pizza topped with fresh spinach, crumbled feta cheese, garlic, and olive oil.
